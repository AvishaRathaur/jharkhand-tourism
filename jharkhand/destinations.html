Top Destinations

Dassam Falls, Ranchi

A spectacular waterfall where the Kanchi River cascades from a height of 144 feet.

Baidyanath Temple, Deoghar

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, this sacred Hindu temple is a major pilgrimage site.

Betla National Park

Home to tigers, elephants, and diverse wildlife, offering thrilling jungle safaris.

Patratu Valley

Known for its winding roads and stunning panoramic views of the dam and hills.

Netarhat

The 'Queen of Chotanagpur', a serene hill station famous for its sunrise and sunset views.

Parasnath Hills

The highest peak in Jharkhand, a revered pilgrimage site for Jains.
