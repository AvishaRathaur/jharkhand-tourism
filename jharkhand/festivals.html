Festivals of Jharkhand

Sarhul

Sarhul is the most important festival for the tribal communities of Jharkhand. It marks the beginning of the new year and is a celebration of nature, with villagers worshipping the Sal tree. The festival is a vibrant affair filled with traditional music, drumming, and energetic group dances. People dress in colorful attire and offer prayers for a bountiful harvest.

Karma

Karma is another significant festival, where people worship the Karma tree for prosperity and good health. A branch of the Karma tree is planted in the village, and people dance around it throughout the night, singing traditional songs. It is a festival of faith, celebration, and community bonding, especially among the youth.

Sohrai

Sohrai is the main festival of the Santhal tribe, celebrated after the harvest. It is a cattle festival where people show gratitude to their cattle for their help in farming. The houses are decorated with beautiful Sohrai paintings, and the cattle are bathed, decorated, and offered special food. The festival is filled with joy, music, and feasting.
