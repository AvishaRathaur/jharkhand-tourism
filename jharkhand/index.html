Welcome to Jharkhand

The Land of Forests

Carved out of Bihar in 2000, Jharkhand is a state brimming with natural beauty. From the dense Sal forests and rolling hills of the Chota Nagpur Plateau to its mesmerizing waterfalls and rich mineral reserves, this land offers a unique blend of nature and indigenous culture. Discover ancient temples, vibrant tribal traditions, and serene landscapes waiting to be explored.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Jharkhand is during the winter months, from October to March, when the weather is cool and pleasant for sightseeing.

Transportation

Ranchi has the main airport connecting to major cities. The state is well-connected by railways. Local buses and taxis are available for internal travel.

Safety & Health

Always carry a basic first-aid kit. It's advisable to drink bottled water. While generally safe, avoid traveling to remote areas after dark.
